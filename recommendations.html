MY FAVORITE ANTHOLOGY EPISODES

By Joe Orange

I watch a lot of anthology shows. Twilight Zone, Outer Limits, Tales from the Darkside, Night Gallery, Hammer House, Fox Mystery Theater, you name it. This is not a comprehensive list. These are simply the episodes that have stuck with me over the years - the ones I think about and the ones I recommend to people who have already seen everything. If you're looking for the same fifteen episodes that appear on every "best of" list, you won't find them here.

Episodes

Anthology 1984

Child's Play

Fox Mystery Theater (Hammer House of Mystery and Suspense)

A small number of people. One location. A problem that makes no sense and keeps getting worse. Mary Crosby, Bing Crosby's daughter, leads a cast dealing with something I won't spoil, except to say it escalates in exactly the right way. A single episode, easy to find, and one that most people have never come across.
