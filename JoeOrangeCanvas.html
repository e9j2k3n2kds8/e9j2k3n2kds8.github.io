A Note from the Creator

I built this canvas as a visual thinking space for writers — somewhere to pin images, sketch out story structure, map character relationships, or just think visually before you start writing. Sometimes a blank page needs a picture first.

It's completely free, runs in your browser, and saves everything locally. No accounts, no cloud, no nonsense.

When I'm not building tools, I write speculative fiction — sci-fi with a touch of horror. You can find my books and everything else at JoeOrange.com.
