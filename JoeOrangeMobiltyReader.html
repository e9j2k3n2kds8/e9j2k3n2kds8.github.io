A Note from the Creator

I had the idea for this program after thinking about people with mobility issues who love to read. Turning a page or scrolling a screen might seem like a small thing, but for some people it's a real barrier. The Mobility Reader takes care of that — it advances the text and turns the pages for you, so all you have to do is sit back and enjoy your book or comic.

The app is completely free and open-source. I hope you find it useful, whether you need hands-free reading or simply prefer a more relaxed way to read.

When I'm not building tools, I write speculative fiction — sci-fi with a touch of horror. If that sounds like your kind of thing, you can find my books and everything else over at JoeOrange.com.
