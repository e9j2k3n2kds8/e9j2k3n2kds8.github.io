About Joe Orange

Joe Orange writes science fiction that explores the boundaries between the ordinary and the impossible. Based in New Zealand, he crafts stories where everyday people encounter extraordinary circumstances that challenge their understanding of reality.

The Strange Detroit series emerged from a fascination with how small anomalies can cascade into world-changing events. Each book stands alone while contributing to a larger tapestry of interconnected mysteries.
