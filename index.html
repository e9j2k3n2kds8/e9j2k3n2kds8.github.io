About

Joe Orange writes science fiction that explores the boundaries between the ordinary and the impossible. Based in New Zealand, he crafts stories where everyday people encounter extraordinary circumstances that challenge their understanding of reality.

The Strange Detroit series emerged from a fascination with how small anomalies can cascade into world-changing events. Each book stands alone while contributing to a larger tapestry of interconnected mysteries.

FAQ

Do I need to read the books in order?

No! Each Strange Detroit book is a standalone story with its own characters and mystery. While they share a universe and certain underlying themes, you can start with whichever book's premise interests you most.

Are the books available in audiobook format?

Yes! The Lift is currently available as an audiobook, narrated by Google's Madison voice. More audiobooks are in production and will be announced through the newsletter.

Will there be more Strange Detroit books?

Absolutely. The Strange Detroit universe has many more stories to tell. Subscribe to the newsletter to stay updated on new releases and behind-the-scenes insights into upcoming books.

Can I get a signed copy?

As the books are currently only available in digital format, signed physical copies aren't available yet. If there's enough interest, print editions may be explored in the future.
